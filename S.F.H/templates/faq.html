How can we help you

Top 10 Frequently asked questions(FAQ)
  • How do I create an account on Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub?

    • You can create your account on Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub by entering and verifying your emial address. Click on "Register" after that and fill up the form to create your Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub account

  • How do I login on Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub? I used to login with email login.

    • For older users of Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub who used to login with email enter your email and validatePassword, then choose your method of login under "Already have an account?". Once you have entered the details, you will be logged in to your account.

  • Do I need to provide email to create an account on Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub ?

    • Yes, Providing an email is mandatory to create an account on Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub. However, Beacuse it will allow you to easily reset your password if you need to.

  • How do I check the status of my order?

    • Please tap on “My Account” section under main menu of MY MyAccount/myorder to check your order status.

  • How can i change my password of Account that I use to login on Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub, that i forgot?

    • You have to go to login page, there is a Option of a forget password. you have to click on that, then just insert your email id and change your password

  • Does Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub read my SMSes?

    • Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub can only read OTP SMSes that are sent by Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub. This is in accordance with guidelines provided by Google and Apple in line with user privacy policies.

  • How does the COD (Cash on Delivery) payment option work?

    • Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub's Cash on Delivery option allows you to pay order value at the time of delivery for all orders between Rs. 299 and Rs. 49999. Your existing limits will be revised once, there is a delivery or order rejected against your account, from the new limit implementation logic, if required.To pay for any order using Cash on Delivery (COD) mode of payment, please select the 'Cash On Delivery' option on the payment page. Cash on Delivery option is available only in selected pincodes.However the COD limit may differ from customer to customer depending upon the order rejection percentage from a particular customers registered email id. For avoidance of doubt, 'Order Rejection Percentage' shall mean any undelivered orders which are returned from the address provided by the customer even, after three successful delivery attempts. In event of COD limit breach, your order will be cancelled and you will have to place a new order using Pre-payment options.

  • I have given my mobile number in my last order? Can I use that number to log in to Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub?

    • Mobile numbers given along with shipping address cannot be used to log in on Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub. To use a number for login on Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub, you need to add your number to your profile and verify it with OTP. This can be done under profile section in Account.

  • Does Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub deliver products outside India?

    • No. At this point, Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub delivers products only within India.

  • How can I pay for my order at Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub?

    • We support the following payment options at Sanskruti_Fashion_Hub: Cash On Delivery (available in selected pin codes) Credit Card Debit Card Net banking
